About

This is a companion app for a strategy board game called Donjon: Rising Towers. This app will provide you with an easy way to keep score, learn the game's rules, and give you detailed descriptions of all the pieces of the game. If you want to report a bug or need to contact us you may reach us at www.DonjonRT@gmail.com.
